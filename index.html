Pros and Cons of Mobile Phones

Pros:-

  • Emergency Services are easily and quickly contacted.
  • We can quickly access safety information.
  • Communication's are very easy.

Cons:-

  • Cell phones are a primary cause of distracted driving.
  • Equipment operations require focus.
  • Productivity and focus may be adversely affected.

NOTE:-There are many Pros and Cons of using Mobile Phone's or having Mobile Phone's. So be careful and stay in limit.
